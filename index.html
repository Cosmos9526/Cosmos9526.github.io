Click Chemistry

Click chemistry is a modular approach to chemical synthesis based on simple, high-yielding reactions. It plays a critical role in bioorthogonal labeling and drug design.

Vaccine Design

Computational vaccine design integrates epitope prediction, structural modeling, and immunoinformatics tools to develop effective and targeted vaccines.

Peptide Design

Peptide therapeutics involve designing amino acid chains that can mimic or inhibit protein-protein interactions, with high specificity and low toxicity.

Antibody Design

Antibody engineering combines computational modeling and molecular biology to create antibodies with enhanced affinity, specificity, and stability.

Molecular Dynamics Simulation

Molecular dynamics simulations provide atomistic insights into the behavior of biomolecules, helping researchers predict conformational changes and binding mechanisms.

Genetic

Genetic engineering in computational biology involves analyzing gene expression data, predicting gene functions, and simulating genetic modifications.
